About me

Hello, I’m Anh and I’m the admin of this blog.

I’ve worked as a [title] for about five years, but my experience started way before that.

I’ve always been interested in writing, storytelling, and helping people tell their stories. When I was a kid, I did a lot of volunteer work with kids who had cancer or other serious illnesses. The experience taught me how important it is to be able to talk with others and listen to their stories.

I believe every person can find their unique voice in the world, which makes us all so unique.
